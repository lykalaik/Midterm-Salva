About Us

In 2020, my husband Bryan and I, Alaika Salva, embarked on an exciting venture fueled by my deep passion for flowers and the art of crafting unique bouquets. We named our business Omorfia Store, drawing inspiration from our beloved daughter's name, Omorfia Zendaya. The journey began amidst the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, pushing us to think innovatively and adapt our business model to the digital landscape.

The years 2020 and 2021 tested our resilience as Bryan and I navigated through the uncertainties of the pandemic. Determined to overcome obstacles, we embraced the opportunity to take our business online, launching our website in 2021. Despite the challenges, the digital platform allowed us to connect with a wider audience and share our passion for flowers with customers beyond our local community.

Through dedication and perseverance, Bryan and I transformed adversity into opportunity, witnessing Omorfia Store blossom into a thriving online destination for exquisite floral creations. Today, I am proud to see our vision come to life, as we continue to spread beauty and joy through our enchanting bouquets, inspired by love, family, and the enduring power of nature.
